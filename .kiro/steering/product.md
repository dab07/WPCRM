# Product Overview

WhatsApp CRM is an AI-powered customer relationship management system designed for WhatsApp business communications. The platform combines automated AI responses with human agent oversight to manage customer conversations at scale.

## Core Features

- **Agentic AI System**: AI-powered conversation handling with confidence scoring and intelligent handover to human agents
- **Conversation Management**: Real-time WhatsApp conversation tracking with status management (active, ai_handled, agent_assigned, closed)
- **Contact Management**: Customer database with tagging, metadata storage, and source tracking
- **Campaign System**: Bulk messaging campaigns with scheduling and targeting capabilities
- **Trigger-Based Automation**: Event-driven actions (keyword detection, inactivity monitoring, tag-based triggers)
- **N8N Workflow Integration**: External workflow automation for lead nurturing, abandoned cart recovery, feedback collection, and re-engagement
- **Follow-up Rules**: Automated follow-up messaging based on time thresholds and conditions
- **AI Intent Recognition**: Keyword-based intent detection with configurable confidence thresholds

## Integration Points

- **Supabase**: PostgreSQL database with REST API for data persistence
- **N8N**: Self-hosted workflow automation engine for complex business logic
- **Gemini AI**: Google's AI for message generation, sentiment analysis, and lead scoring
- **WhatsApp Business API**: Message delivery and receipt (integration layer not included in codebase)
